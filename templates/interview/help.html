Frequently Asked Questions

  2. AI extracts your skills - Our system analyzes your resume and identifies your technical skills
  3. Start practicing - Begin mock interviews tailored to your skill set
  4. Get feedback - Receive detailed feedback and improve your interview skills

Nexora's AI analyzes your resume to extract:
  • Technical Skills - Programming languages, frameworks, tools, databases
  • Experience - Years of professional experience and job titles
  • Education - Degrees, institutions, and fields of study
  • Contact Information - Email, phone, LinkedIn, GitHub profiles
  • Skill Categories - Organized into Web Dev, AI/ML, Cloud, Security, etc.

Your data is encrypted and never shared with third parties.

Nexora uses a fixed distribution model for consistent practice:

  • 3 Beginner level questions
  • 3 Intermediate level questions
  • 3 Advanced/Hard level questions

Questions are matched to your skill set from the resume analysis, ensuring relevant practice for your background.

Your answers are evaluated using AI-powered keyword matching:

  • Keyword Analysis - Checks for technical terms and concepts
  • Completeness - Evaluates coverage of key points
  • Level Assessment - Determines if you should progress to harder questions

You'll receive detailed feedback showing which keywords you covered and areas for improvement.
